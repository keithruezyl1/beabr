# Product Brief
# Beabr — Prepare Smarter Gifts

## 1. Product Summary

Beabr is a graduation registry web app that helps graduates organize gift requests and helps friends or family prepare gifts without duplication.

The product allows a graduate to create a registry, add desired gifts or money pledge funds, and invite people through a code or link. Gift givers can reserve or prepare items privately. The graduate can only see who gave what after a scheduled reveal date and time.

---

## 2. Product Name

**Beabr**

---

## 3. Tagline

**Prepare Smarter Gifts**

---

## 4. Mascot

The mascot is a beaver.

The beaver represents:

- preparation
- building
- gathering
- reliability
- thoughtfulness

The mascot should support the brand visually but should not make the app feel childish.

---

## 5. Problem

Graduation gifting is often uncoordinated.

Common problems:

- Multiple people buy the same gift
- The graduate receives things they do not need
- Gift givers do not know what has already been prepared
- Money contributions are hard to track
- Surprise is difficult to preserve
- Thank-you follow-up is manual and easy to forget

---

## 6. Solution

Beabr gives graduates a private registry where they can list desired items and let invited people prepare gifts or pledge money.

The app hides giver identities until a reveal date/time chosen by the graduate.

After reveal, the graduate can send in-app thank-you letters to each gift giver.

---

## 7. Core Value Proposition

Beabr helps people prepare better graduation gifts by making gift coordination simple, private, and organized.

---

## 8. Primary Users

### Registry Owner

A graduating person who wants to organize gift preferences.

### Gift Giver

A person invited to prepare a gift or pledge money.

---

## 9. Product Positioning

Beabr is not a social network.

It is a private registry and gift-preparation tracker with the polish and ease of a premium modern app.

---

## 10. Key Features

- Google OAuth login
- Create graduation registry
- Join registry with code/link
- Add gift items
- Add cash pledge funds
- Reserve gifts
- Mark gifts as prepared
- Track quantity fulfillment
- Hide contributors before reveal
- Reveal contributors at scheduled date/time
- Send thank-you letters after reveal
- Show in-app thank-you pop-up to givers

---

## 11. Differentiators

### Scheduled Reveal

Gift giver identities remain hidden until the owner’s chosen reveal date/time.

### Gift-Preparation Focus

The app tracks gift preparation, not payment processing.

### Thank-You Loop

After reveal, the owner can send thank-you messages that appear in-app for each giver.

### Premium Utility UX

The product should feel polished and modern without becoming a social platform.

---

## 12. Early iteration product principles

1. Keep actions simple.
2. Protect surprise before reveal.
3. Prevent duplicate gifts.
4. Make item status obvious.
5. Make the app feel premium but lightweight.
6. Avoid social features.
7. Keep payment integrations out of the early iteration.

---

## 13. Early iteration scope

This early iteration includes:

- Web app experience
- Hosted application with a modern web client
- Durable storage for registry records
- Supported sign-in flows including Google where enabled
- Registry creation
- Gift item tracking
- Cash pledge tracking
- Scheduled reveal
- Thank-you messages

---

- organized
- warm
- polished
- trustworthy

- thoughtful

- childish
- loud
- overly playful
- like a social network

---

## 16. One-Sentence Pitch

Beabr is a private graduation registry that helps friends and family prepare smarter gifts while keeping the surprise until reveal day.

---

## 17. Primary User Journey

1. Graduate creates registry.
2. Graduate adds items and pledge funds.
3. Graduate shares code/link.
4. Gift givers join.
5. Gift givers reserve or prepare gifts.
6. Graduate tracks progress without seeing identities.
7. Reveal date arrives.
8. Graduate sees who gave what.
9. Graduate sends thank-you letters.
10. Gift givers see thank-you notes in-app.

---

## 18. Success criteria

This early iteration is successful if users can:

- Create a registry quickly
- Add gifts easily
- Share access without confusion
- Prevent duplicate gifts
- Preserve surprise before reveal
- Reveal gift contributors clearly
- Send thank-you messages after reveal
