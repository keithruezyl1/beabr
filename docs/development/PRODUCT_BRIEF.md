# Product Brief
# Beabr - Prepare Smarter Gifts

## 1. Product Summary

Beabr is a gift registry web app that helps people organize gift ideas for milestones and helps friends or family prepare thoughtful gifts without duplication.

The product allows a registry owner to create a registry, add gift ideas or money pledge funds, and invite loved ones through a code or link. Gift givers can reserve or prepare items. Each registry uses one immutable visibility mode chosen during setup: **private surprise** or **open coordination**.

---

## 2. Product Name

**Beabr**

---

## 3. Tagline

**Prepare Smarter Gifts**

---

## 4. Mascot

The mascot is a beaver. The beaver represents preparation, building, gathering, reliability, and thoughtfulness.

The mascot should support the brand visually but should not make the app feel childish.

---

## 5. Problem

Gifting for milestones is often uncoordinated.

Common problems:

- Multiple people buy the same gift
- The celebrated person receives things they do not need
- Gift givers do not know what has already been prepared
- Money contributions are hard to track
- Some groups want surprise, while others need open coordination
- Thank-you follow-up is manual and easy to forget

---

## 6. Solution

Beabr gives people a private registry where they can list meaningful gift ideas and let invited loved ones prepare gifts or pledge money.

**Private surprise** hides giver identities until reveal. **Open coordination** shows attribution earlier so loved ones can coordinate clearly and avoid duplicate gifts. The visibility choice is made during setup and cannot be changed later.

After reveal, the registry owner can send in-app thank-you letters to each gift giver.

---

## 7. Core Value Proposition

Beabr helps people prepare better gifts by making coordination simple, warm, private where chosen, and organized.

---

## 8. Primary Users

### Registry Owner

A person planning a milestone who wants to share gift preferences clearly.

### Gift Giver

A person invited to prepare a gift or pledge money.

---

## 9. Product Positioning

Beabr is not a social network. It is a private registry and gift-preparation tracker with the polish and ease of a premium modern app.

---

## 10. Key Features

- Google OAuth login
- Create registry
- Choose immutable gift visibility during setup
- Join registry with code/link
- Add gift items
- Add cash pledge funds
- Reserve gifts
- Mark gifts as prepared
- Track quantity fulfillment
- Hide contributors before reveal in private surprise mode
- Show attribution immediately in open coordination mode
- Reveal contributors at scheduled date/time
- Send thank-you letters after reveal
- Show in-app thank-you pop-up to givers

---

## 11. Differentiators

### Scheduled Reveal

Gift giver identities remain hidden until the owner's chosen reveal date/time in private surprise mode.

### Open Coordination

Loved ones can see who reserved, prepared, initiated, or contributed so shared gifting is easier to coordinate.

### Gift-Preparation Focus

The app tracks gift preparation, not payment processing.

### Thank-You Loop

After reveal, the owner can send thank-you messages that appear in-app for each giver.

### Premium Utility UX

The product should feel polished and modern without becoming a social platform.

---

## 12. Early iteration product principles

1. Keep actions simple.
2. Protect surprise before reveal when private surprise is chosen.
3. Prevent duplicate gifts.
4. Make item status obvious.
5. Make the app feel premium but lightweight.
6. Avoid social features.
7. Keep payment integrations out of the early iteration.
8. Keep copy love-focused: the registry helps people choose thoughtful gifts for someone they care about.

---

## 13. Early iteration scope

This early iteration includes:

- Web app experience
- Hosted application with a modern web client
- Durable storage for registry records
- Supported sign-in flows including Google where enabled
- Registry creation
- Gift item tracking
- Cash pledge tracking
- Scheduled reveal
- Thank-you messages

---

## 14. Future Version Ideas

Future versions may include GCash connection, bank connection, payment proof upload, real payment status tracking, email reminders, push notifications, gift recommendations, multiple registries, registry templates, and event-specific registry types.

---

## 15. Brand Personality

Beabr should feel helpful, organized, warm, polished, trustworthy, calm, and thoughtful.

It should not feel childish, loud, overly playful, like a social network, like a payment app, or like a marketplace.

---

## 16. One-Sentence Pitch

Beabr helps friends and family prepare thoughtful gifts with clear coordination, optional surprise, and an easier thank-you loop.

---

## 17. Primary User Journey

1. Registry owner creates registry.
2. Registry owner chooses private surprise or open coordination.
3. Registry owner adds items and pledge funds.
4. Registry owner shares code/link.
5. Gift givers join.
6. Gift givers reserve or prepare gifts.
7. Registry owner tracks progress according to the chosen visibility mode.
8. Reveal date arrives.
9. Registry owner sees protected attribution where reveal applies.
10. Registry owner sends thank-you letters.
11. Gift givers see thank-you notes in-app.

---

## 18. Success criteria

This early iteration is successful if users can:

- Create a registry quickly
- Add gifts easily
- Share access without confusion
- Prevent duplicate gifts
- Preserve surprise before reveal when private surprise is chosen
- Support open coordination when loved ones need clearer gift planning
- Reveal gift contributors clearly
- Send thank-you messages after reveal
