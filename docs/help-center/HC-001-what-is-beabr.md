# What is Beabr?

**Article ID:** HC-001  
**Audience:** Customers (everyone)

Beabr is a private gift preparation registry for occasions like graduations, weddings, and celebrations. It helps people choose thoughtful gifts for someone they love while reducing duplicates and confusion.

---

## What you can do here

If you are planning a milestone, Beabr lets you share gift ideas, helpful details, and optional money goals with invited loved ones.

If you are gifting, Beabr lets you:

- Browse the gifts the **Registry Owner** listed
- Reserve or mark items prepared
- Optionally participate in pooled money coordination for bigger gifts, where enabled
- Coordinate with privacy based on the registry's chosen gift visibility mode

Registries use one gift visibility mode chosen during setup:

- **Private surprise:** names tied to reservations, preparation, and item-level pledges stay hidden from the **Registry Owner** until reveal.
- **Open coordination:** invited loved ones can see who reserved, prepared, initiated, or contributed so the group can coordinate more clearly.

After reveal, the **Registry Owner** can recognize contributors and send thank-you notes inside the app.

---

## Important: Beabr is not your bank or wallet

Unless we clearly ship a regulated payment integration with separate legal disclosures:

- Beabr does not collect, hold, or send money as a licensed payment service.
- Transfers typically happen outside Beabr, for example at your bank, wallet app, store checkout, or in person.
- Amounts you log in Beabr are mainly for coordination and record keeping, not proof that funds cleared at a bank.

Always verify payment details yourself before sending money. Read **[Terms of use](../legal/terms-of-service.md)** for pooled pledges and liability disclaimers.

---

## Privacy and coordination

Gift preparation can be quiet or open, depending on what the **Registry Owner** chooses at creation. That choice cannot be changed later.

For details see **[Privacy before and after reveal](HC-030-privacy-reveal-summary.md)** plus **[Privacy overview](../legal/privacy-policy.md)**.

Sign-in is hosted for you, commonly with Google plus email with a one time code, without a reusable Beabr-only password for that usual path. See **[Signing in](HC-002-sign-in.md)**.
