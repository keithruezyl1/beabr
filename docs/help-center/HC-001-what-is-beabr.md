# What is Beabr?

**Article ID:** HC-001  
**Audience:** Customers (everyone)

Beabr is a **private gift preparation registry** for occasions like graduations and celebrations; it is neither a social network nor a general public storefront.

---

## What you can do here

If you are planning a milestone, Beabr lets you organize a registry of hoped-for gifts so friends and family **see what still needs preparing** instead of doubling up by accident.

If you are gifting, Beabr lets you:

- Browse what the **Registry Owner** listed  
- Reserve or mark items  
- Optionally participate in pooled money coordination for bigger gifts (**where enabled**)  
- Keep certain details private until the **Registry Owner’s** reveal time  

After reveal (whenever the **Registry Owner** scheduled it), the **Registry Owner** can recognize contributors and send thank you notes **inside the app**.

---

## Important: Beabr is not your bank or wallet

Unless we clearly ship a regulated payment integration with separate legal disclosures:

- Beabr **does not** collect, hold, or send money as a licensed payment service.  
- Transfers typically happen **outside** Beabr (for example at your bank, e wallet app, store checkout, or cash in person).  
- Amounts you log in Beabr are mainly for **coordination and record keeping** among people who chose to cooperate, not proof that funds cleared at a bank.  

Always verify payment details yourself before sending money. Read **[Terms of use](../legal/terms-of-service.md)** for pooled pledges and liability disclaimers.

---

## Privacy at the core

Gift preparation can be surprising. **Registry Owners** usually **cannot** see who picked which gift or who chipped in until reveal. Rules are enforced on the server, not merely hidden in the user interface.

For details see **[Privacy before and after reveal](HC-030-privacy-reveal-summary.md)** plus **[Privacy overview](../legal/privacy-policy.md)**.

---

## Signing in

Sign-in is hosted for you (commonly **Google** plus **email with a one time code**), without a reusable Beabr-only password for that usual path. See **[Signing in](HC-002-sign-in.md)**.
