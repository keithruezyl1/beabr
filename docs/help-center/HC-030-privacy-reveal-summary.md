# Privacy before and after reveal (summary)

**Article ID:** HC-030  
**Audience:** Everyone

This is a short summary layered under **[Privacy overview](../legal/privacy-policy.md)** and **[Terms of use](../legal/terms-of-service.md)**.

---

## Why reveal exists

Many **Registry Owners** want generosity **without spoilers** (“who picked what”) until a chosen moment near the celebration.

---

## What reveal changes

### Before reveal

- **Registry Owners** can manage items and see quantity progress.  
- **Registry Owners** do not see **Registry Participant** identity tied to gift reservations and preparation, or item-level pooled pledge participation, until reveal.  
- Cash fund totals before reveal follow the visibility setting described in **[Pledge and total visibility settings](HC-031-pledge-visibility.md)**.  

### After reveal

Contributor attribution becomes visible to **Registry Owners** for protected categories so thank you notes and closure are possible. See **[After reveal](HC-014-after-reveal.md)**.

---

## Registry Participants’ reciprocal duties

**Registry Participants** must not circumvent privacy safeguards or harass others leveraging partial knowledge.

See the **Supplemental reminders by role** section in **[Terms of use](../legal/terms-of-service.md)** (**Registry Participants**).

---

## Data related to payments

Some flows store **explicit payout details and receipts**. Those carry **elevated confidentiality rules** irrespective of classical “reveal countdown” narration because bank-like artifacts need continual protection.

Read **[Group pledges and sensitive payout information](HC-024-pledges-group-gifts-sensitive-info.md)**.
