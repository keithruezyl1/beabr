# Privacy before and after reveal (summary)

**Article ID:** HC-030  
**Audience:** Everyone

This is a short summary layered under **[Privacy overview](../legal/privacy-policy.md)** and **[Terms of use](../legal/terms-of-service.md)**.

---

## Why reveal exists

Many **Registry Owners** want generosity **without spoilers** ("who picked what") until a chosen moment near the celebration. Others prefer open coordination so loved ones can avoid duplicate gifts and know where help is still needed.

---

## Gift visibility modes

The **Registry Owner** chooses one mode during setup. It cannot be changed after the registry is created.

- **Private surprise:** before reveal, **Registry Owners** can manage items and see quantity progress, but do not see **Registry Participant** identity tied to gift reservations, preparation, or item-level pooled pledge participation.
- **Open coordination:** attribution can be visible before reveal to invited members, including who reserved, prepared, initiated a group pledge, or contributed to one. This is for groups that want clearer coordination and fewer duplicate gifts.

Cash fund totals before reveal follow the visibility setting described in **[Pledge and total visibility settings](HC-031-pledge-visibility.md)**.

Gift visibility is immutable after setup. If the group needs a different coordination style, create a new registry with the preferred mode.

---

## After reveal

Contributor attribution becomes visible to **Registry Owners** for protected private surprise categories so thank-you notes and closure are possible. See **[After reveal](HC-014-after-reveal.md)**.

---

## Registry Participants' reciprocal duties

**Registry Participants** must not circumvent privacy safeguards or harass others leveraging partial knowledge. In open coordination registries, use attribution only to coordinate thoughtful gifts and avoid duplicates.

See the **Supplemental reminders by role** section in **[Terms of use](../legal/terms-of-service.md)** (**Registry Participants**).

---

## Data related to payments

Some flows store **explicit payout details and receipts**. Those carry **elevated confidentiality rules** irrespective of reveal or open coordination settings because bank-like artifacts need continual protection.

Read **[Group pledges and sensitive payout information](HC-024-pledges-group-gifts-sensitive-info.md)**.
