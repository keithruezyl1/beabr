# Private notes

**Article ID:** HC-023  
**Audience:** Registry Participants

Some flows let you attach **notes** to reservations or related records, often described as “private” in the product.

---

## What “private” means here

**Private** in Beabr means **routine product privacy rules**. For example, a **Registry Owner** may **not** read certain notes **before reveal**, consistent with enforced product behavior.

**Private does not promise absolute secrecy in every scenario**, including:

- lawful government requests validated through legal process compelling the developer  
- insider threats or catastrophic security breaches (no online service reduces risk to zero)  
- your own device compromise  

For what Beabr is responsible for when misuse or security issues occur, read **[Beabr’s responsibility](../legal/beabr-responsibility.md)**.

So **avoid storing banking passwords**, government ID numbers, or intimate safety-planning secrets solely inside note fields. Use appropriately secure channels when stakes are high.

**Registry Owners:** after reveal expands visibility surfaces, remain ethical about quoting notes.

---

## Etiquette

Notes should help **you** coordinate (sizes, retailer chosen, duplication avoidance hints devoid of coercion), not bully others.
