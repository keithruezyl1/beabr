# Common sign-in problems

**Article ID:** HC-040  
**Audience:** Customers

These tips apply when your Beabr instance offers **Sign in with Google** plus **email one time passwords**.

---

## Browser blocks sign-in pop-ups

Allow pop-ups for your Beabr hostname or repeat the flow if the product falls back to full-page redirects during the release you run.

---

## Unclear which Google account is active

Sign out at Google centrally if multiple Workspace or consumer profiles might have attached to the browser before returning to Beabr.

---

## Email code issues

Request a fresh code after old codes expire. Inspect spam folders. Delivery speed depends on your provider and how the developer configured outbound mail.

---

## Device clock far from real time

Incorrect system time can interfere with session cookies. Fix automatic time sync on the device.

---

## Managed enterprise browsers

Some organizations block sign-in to third-party identity providers. Resolve with your IT team using the domains your developer lists for this app.

---

## Session ended without an on-screen warning

Sign in again. Unsaved in app draft text may disappear when the session drops.

---

## Still blocked

You may contact **keith_tagarao01@yahoo.com.ph** and describe what is blocking sign in.

Include:

- The approximate time you saw it (UTC if you can)  
- Your browser and operating system  
- Any error text you saw on screen  

You can also report suspected phishing sites that copy Beabr visuals to the same inbox.
