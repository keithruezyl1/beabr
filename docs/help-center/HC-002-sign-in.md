# Signing in

**Article ID:** HC-002  
**Privacy:** Categories of data collected are listed in **[Privacy overview](../legal/privacy-policy.md)**.

Beabr uses a **hosted sign-in service** configured for your instance. You ordinarily do not create a separate Beabr-only password for the common **email one time code** path.

---

## Supported sign-in methods

What you see depends on how your Beabr host configured sign-in. Typical options:

1. **Sign in with Google.** You complete Google’s sign-in flow in the browser, then return to Beabr with an active session.
2. **Email plus one time code.** You enter an email address; a short code is sent to that inbox. Enter the code in the Beabr screen before it expires.

Your relationship with **Google**, your **email provider**, and the **identity host** remains governed by those parties’ notices.

---

## What Beabr uses from your sign-in

Examples include:

- An internal account identifier the product needs to tie registries and actions to you  
- Profile fields such as name, email, and optional photo when the provider supplies them  
- Timestamps such as last sign-in when the product surfaces them  

Reveal timing and pledge privacy rules follow this identity binding.
