# Pledge and total visibility settings

**Article ID:** HC-031  
**Audience:** Registry Owners and Registry Participants

Gift registries may mix:

- Line item gift availability
- Cash fund blocks with textual instructions
- Group pooled pledges on an item, with **Pledge Initiator** and contributing **Registry Participant** workflows

Each can have subtly different visibility before reveal. Always trust your live screens.

---

## Cash fund aggregates

**Registry Owners** sometimes control whether overall pledged totals per fund are visible before reveal without exposing individual payer identities.

If enabled, owners may infer momentum, such as "we are halfway," while still not knowing granular donors until reveal unless the product explicitly shows that attribution.

If disabled, totals may be withheld until reveal for stricter secrecy.

---

## Item-level group pledges

**Registry Participants** looking at eligible items may see gathered pool sums aiding coordination.

**Registry Owner** identity visibility follows the registry's setup-time gift visibility mode:

- **Private surprise:** owner attribution for item-level pledge initiators and contributors stays protected until reveal.
- **Open coordination:** attribution may show earlier so loved ones can coordinate a shared gift.

Payout instructions and receipt artifacts remain sensitive in both modes. Open coordination is not permission to expose QR images, receipts, payout fields, encrypted fields, storage paths, or private notes as general registry details.

---

## Why settings differ by feature type

Funds versus item pools use different data and privacy needs. Do not assume a cash-fund total setting automatically changes structured pledge attribution. The product should label clearly.

---

## Consumer transparency principle

Misleading dashboards implying funds are safely escrowed internally when they are actually only logs harms fairness. Marketing and UI should mirror **[Terms of use](../legal/terms-of-service.md)**.
