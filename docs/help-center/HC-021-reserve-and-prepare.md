# Reserve and prepare a gift item

**Article ID:** HC-021  
**Audience:** Registry Participants

This article explains what it means to **reserve** a gift item and what it means to mark it **prepared**.

Reserving tells the gift group: "I am aiming to prepare this portion so we do not all buy the same thing."

**Prepared** means you are signaling that you finished your side of the gift: bought it, crafted it, ordered shipping, or otherwise completed the commitment.

---

## Reserve

1. Open an item showing available quantity.
2. Choose how many units you wish to reserve.
3. Submit. The app reflects your reservation and reduces remaining availability.

If an item already hit reserved/prepared limits, you cannot take more unless someone cancels appropriately or the owner increases the quantity.

---

## Mark prepared

1. Find your active reservation on the item.
2. Choose **Mark as prepared**.
3. Confirm if the app asks.

This helps other loved ones understand what is already handled.

---

## Who can see your name

Visibility depends on the registry's setup-time gift visibility choice:

- **Private surprise:** your name is hidden from the **Registry Owner** until reveal for protected gift actions.
- **Open coordination:** your name may be visible right away to the owner and other invited participants so the group can coordinate.

Private preparation notes are not part of general coordination visibility.

---

## Honesty and accuracy

- Reserve only amounts you sincerely intend. Mass locking items wastes others' goodwill and may violate **[Terms of use](../legal/terms-of-service.md)**.
- Only mark prepared when it is substantively true under your honest understanding.

---

## Cancellations

If plans change, use any cancel flow offered so others can pick up availability. If cancellation is blocked by policy, coordinate respectfully through ordinary channels.

---

## Relationship to money pledges

Some items may support group pooled money instead of or alongside single-buyer prep.

- If an item has a pooled pledge initiated, other participants typically cannot reserve that item and can only participate by pledging.
- Preparing a physical gift differs from logging transferred money for pooled gifts.

See **[Pledge rules for gift items](HC-025-pledge-rules.md)** and **[Group pledges and sensitive payout information](HC-024-pledges-group-gifts-sensitive-info.md)**.
