# Reserve and prepare a gift item

**Article ID:** HC-021  
**Audience:** Registry Participants

This article explains what it means to **reserve** a gift item and what it means to mark it **prepared**.

Reserving tells the **Registry Owner** and other **Registry Participants**: “I’m aiming to prepare **this portion** so we don’t all buy the same thing.”

“Prepared” means you are signaling you **finished** your side of the gift: bought it, crafted it, ordered shipping, or otherwise completed the commitment consistent with your reservation.

---

## Reserve (typical flow)

1. Open an item showing **available** quantity.  
2. Choose **how many** units you wish to reserve (within limits).  
3. Submit. The app should reflect your reservation and reduce remaining availability (**enforced in the live product**, not only on your screen).  

If an item already hit **reserved/prepared** limits, you cannot take more unless someone cancels appropriately.

---

## Mark prepared (typical flow)

1. Find your **active reservation** on the item.  
2. Choose **Mark as prepared** (or equivalent).  
3. Confirm if the app asks. This may affect availability math for other **Registry Participants**.  

Exact labels follow your build.

---

## Honesty and accuracy

- Reserve only amounts you sincerely intend. **Weaponized mass locking** wastes others’ goodwill and may violate norms described under **[acceptable use in Terms](../legal/terms-of-service.md)**.
- Only mark prepared when it is **substantively true** under your honest understanding. False signals harm planning and trust.

---

## Cancellations

If plans change, use any **cancel** flow offered so others can pick up availability. If cancellation is blocked by policy, contact your circle or **Registry Owner** through ordinary channels **outside** harassment vectors.

---

## Private notes

You may be able to add **private preparation notes**. Visibility rules depend on reveal state and product design. See **[Private notes](HC-023-private-notes.md)**.

---

## Relationship to money pledges

Some items may support **group pooled money** instead of or alongside single-buyer prep.

- If an item has a pooled pledge initiated, other participants typically **cannot reserve** that item and can only participate by **pledging**. See **[Pledge rules for gift items](HC-025-pledge-rules.md)**.
- Preparing a **physical gift** differs from logging **transferred money** for pooled gifts. For money and receipt uploads, see **[Group pledges and sensitive payout information](HC-024-pledges-group-gifts-sensitive-info.md)**.

Note: pooled pledges are not the same as gift reservations. Once a pooled pledge is started, it cannot be cancelled in Beabr.
