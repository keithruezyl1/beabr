# Add and organize gift items

**Article ID:** HC-010  
**Audience:** Registry Owners

Gift items help invited loved ones understand what would be meaningful, useful, or welcome for the person being celebrated.

---

## What goes into an item

Depending on categories and product version, you can usually include fields such as:

- **Gift name** and **category**
- **Description** and instructions for **Registry Participants**
- **Quantity needed**
- **Price reference** as a helpful hint, not a checkout total in the baseline product
- **Store**, **external link**, **preferred option**, **alternatives**
- **Photos**

Category-specific attributes, like size or color, may appear as flexible fields.

---

## How availability works

For each item, Beabr tracks how much is:

- **Reserved**: quantity still in reserved status
- **Prepared**: quantity marked prepared
- **Claimed**: reserved plus prepared
- **Available**: quantity still open to reserve

**Registry Participants** cannot over-reserve beyond what remains available. The running product enforces that.

### Status labels Registry Participants see

The product maps those counts to five participant-facing status labels:

1. **Available**: no quantity is reserved or prepared yet.
2. **Prepared**: prepared quantity meets or covers quantity needed.
3. **Partially Prepared**: at least one unit is prepared, but not the full quantity.
4. **Reserved**: nothing is prepared yet, and reserved quantity covers quantity needed.
5. **Partially Reserved**: nothing is prepared yet, and some quantity is reserved.

### What Registry Owners see

Owner views emphasize the numeric breakdown: reserved, prepared, and available. If the registry uses **open coordination**, attribution can also show who reserved or prepared so loved ones can coordinate gifts more clearly. If the registry uses **private surprise**, those names stay hidden from the owner until reveal.

---

## Archiving an item

Owners may archive a gift item while no one has an active reservation, no one has marked it prepared, and no group pledge has been started for it.

Once someone has reserved, prepared, or initiated a group pledge, the item stays available in the registry record so the group does not lose coordination context.

---

## Group pledges on an item

For some gifts, **Registry Participants** may coordinate pooled contributions alongside or instead of a single purchaser. That involves information related to external payments, initiated by **Registry Participants**, not by you as the **Registry Owner** in typical flows, and settlements happen outside Beabr.

For the behavioral rules, see **[Pledge rules for gift items](HC-025-pledge-rules.md)**.

Read **[Group pledges and sensitive payout information](HC-024-pledges-group-gifts-sensitive-info.md)** before inviting people to use that path.
