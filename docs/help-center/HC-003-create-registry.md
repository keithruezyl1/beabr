# Create your first registry

**Article ID:** HC-003  
**Audience:** Registry Owners

This guide walks through creating a registry as the **Registry Owner** organizing gifts for an event (for example a graduation).

---

## Before you start

- You need whichever sign in method your Beabr instance offers (often Google plus email one time codes).
- Choose a **gift visibility** style. **Private surprise** keeps contributor names hidden until reveal; **Open coordination** lets invited loved ones see who reserved, prepared, initiated, or contributed right away. This choice is made during setup and cannot be changed later.
- Think about your **reveal date and time**. For private surprise registries, it controls when hidden contributor details unlock. For open coordination registries, it still marks the celebration/reveal moment.
- Have a **title** and short **message** ready for **Registry Participants** you invite.

---

## Steps to create a registry

1. **Sign in** to Beabr using the supported method shown on screen.
2. Open **Create registry** (or equivalent in your app version).
3. Fill in **registry details**:
   - Title (how the registry appears to **Registry Participants**)
   - Your **display name** as the **Registry Owner**
   - Optional message for **Registry Participants**
   - **Event / graduation date** if you use it
   - **Gift visibility**, chosen once during setup
   - **Reveal date and time**, when hidden contributor information can unlock for private surprise registries
4. **Save** and note your **join code** and **share link** (see [Share your registry](HC-011-share-registry.md)).
5. **Add gift items** (see [Add and organize gift items](HC-010-gift-items.md)) and optional **cash fund** sections if your instance includes them.

Exact field names and optional images (cover photo) depend on the product version you are using.

---

## Your responsibilities as the Registry Owner

- List items **truthfully** and keep instructions clear.
- Protect your **join code** like an invite: anyone with it may be able to join unless you rotate or restrict access as the product allows.
- Understand when attribution is visible. In open coordination registries, names can be visible before reveal so loved ones can avoid duplicates and coordinate thoughtful gifts. Handle that information respectfully and only for gift coordination or thank-you messages.
- Comply with **local laws** that might apply to fundraising, school rules, taxes, or workplace policies. Beabr is software, not legal advice.
