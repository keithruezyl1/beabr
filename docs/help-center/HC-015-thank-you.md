# Send a thank you note

**Article ID:** HC-015  
**Audience:** Registry Owners

After reveal, Beabr supports **thank you messages inside the app** so you can acknowledge people who reserved items, prepared gifts, or contributed in ways the product attributes to them.

---

## Basics

- Thank you messages are **not email campaigns** in this early iteration. They are **inside the Beabr experience** for recipients when they return.  
- Keep messages **truthful, kind, and lawful**: no coercion, threats, deceptive claims, or harassment.  
- Respect that recipients may read messages on shared devices; avoid embedding highly sensitive secrets.  

---

## Draft vs sent vs seen

The app uses these statuses:

- **Draft**: saved but not sent yet.  
- **Sent**: delivered to the recipient’s inbox in the app.  
- **Seen**: the recipient opened it.  

Timestamps help you keep track of what you already sent.

---

## If you cannot thank everyone equally

If you are short on time, a simple message is still valuable. Keep it respectful and avoid pressure, guilt, or manipulation.

---

## Related legal norms

Misleading thank you content tied to future purchases or donation pressure could breach consumer protection concepts in some jurisdictions. Stay straightforward.
