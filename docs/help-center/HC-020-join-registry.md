# Join a registry with a code or link

**Article ID:** HC-020  
**Audience:** Registry Participants

You become a **Registry Participant** when a **Registry Owner** organizing an event shares a **join code** or **link** with you.

---

## Steps

1. **Sign in** using **Google** **or** the email one time code flow when both are enabled for you.  
2. Open the **Join** screen (from the menu or the link you received).  
3. Either:  
   - Paste or type the **code**, or  
   - Follow the **shared URL** (it may pre-fill the code).  
4. Confirm you are joining the **correct** registry. Titles should match what your host told you.  

---

## Privacy expectations

- You will see **gift items** (and possibly cash fund guidance) shared by the **Registry Owner**.  
- Before reveal, the product limits identity visibility for some actions so surprises stay protected. Do not try to bypass that design.  
- Your own reservations, notes visible to you, and contribution statuses appear in **your** views.  

See **[Privacy before and after reveal](HC-030-privacy-reveal-summary.md)**.
