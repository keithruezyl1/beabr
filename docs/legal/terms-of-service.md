## What Beabr is

Beabr coordinates private gift lists, reservations, optional cash bookkeeping, reveal schedules, thank you notes inside the application, pooled pledge facilitation where enabled, and membership rules **enforced by the hosted service** (not only by what a browser screen shows). Typical deployments offer **Sign in with Google** and **email address with a one time sign in code**. Beabr does not maintain a separate reusable password for that common email-code path.

Your relationship with **Google**, your **email provider**, or any **identity host** the developer configures remains governed by their own terms when you authenticate.

## What Beabr is not

Beabr is not a bank, wallet, payment processor, escrow agent, or money transmitter. Amounts stored as pledges or manual entries are coordination records, not proof that funds cleared through a licensed payment rail integrated into Beabr unless a future release says otherwise plainly.

Transfers of actual money normally happen outside the application. Registry Participants verify counterparties directly with banks or wallets before sending funds.

Structured payout fields, scan to pay QR reference files, receipt photographs, notes, or similar uploads can constitute highly sensitive financial data. Enter only what the moment requires.
